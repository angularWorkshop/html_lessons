Chef's Special: Carbonara Recipe

Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente Fry guanciale until crispy Mix eggs, pecorino, and black pepper in a bowl Drain pasta and toss with guanciale off the heat Add egg mixture and toss quickly to create a creamy sauce Serve immediately with extra pecorino
