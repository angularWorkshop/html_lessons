Chef's Special: Carbonara Recipe

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente
  2. Fry guanciale until crispy
  3. Mix eggs, pecorino, and black pepper in a bowl
  4. Drain pasta and toss with guanciale off the heat
  5. Add egg mixture and toss quickly to create a creamy sauce
  6. Serve immediately with extra pecorino
